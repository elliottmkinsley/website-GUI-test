Welcome to the NAU Radiant Center Dedicated to advancing remote sensing technology and applications.

The Radiant Center for Remote Sensing brings together professionals from various disciplines to advance remote sensing technology and applications.

From planetary furfaces and atmospheres to forest, wildfire, water and urban enviornments, our team is dedicated to furthering our understanding of earth and beyond.
